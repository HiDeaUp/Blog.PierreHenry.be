Feeling happy, feeling good—this is what we all want, isn’t it? The real goal of life is to be happy. When you’re happy, everything else falls into place. Tasks seem easier, and you can truly be in the moment. But when unhappiness creeps in, everything feels heavier. There’s a weight on your chest, a mental load that makes it hard to enjoy anything. You can’t really appreciate the good things around you because you’re not feeling well inside. It’s like a fog that keeps you from being fully present.

On the other hand, when you’re happy, everything becomes so much easier. It’s like putting on a new pair of glasses—suddenly, you see the world differently. You enjoy things more, you smile more, and you say “yes” to new opportunities without hesitation. Happiness makes you open to life. People notice your smile, and they’re more likely to offer you new chances. You become less guarded, less suspicious, and more willing to trust others.

Often, when we’re unhappy or not comfortable in our own skin, we’re afraid to say yes to things. We hesitate, we’re on the defensive. But when you feel good, when you’re truly comfortable with yourself, trust comes naturally. And here’s something important: most people on this planet are kind. Maybe not everyone, but I’d say 90%, even 95% of people are good-hearted and caring.

So, what I wish for you is simple: start now. Look for what you can do to feel better, even just a little. Happiness isn’t some distant dream—it’s something you can reach for today.

## Key Takeaways
- Happiness makes everything easier and brighter.
- When you’re happy, you’re more open to new opportunities and trust others more.
- Most people are kind—don’t let fear or unhappiness close you off.
- Start today: do one thing that makes you feel good.

## Reflection
- What small action can you take right now to feel a bit happier?
- Are you letting a mental fog keep you from enjoying the present?
- How would your life change if you trusted others a little more?
