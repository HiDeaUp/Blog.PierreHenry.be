Pushing yourself beyond your limits is not just a motivational slogan. It is a way of living that can completely change what you believe is possible for you. When you challenge yourself and aim higher, you discover new strengths, and you start to see what you are truly capable of achieving.

One of the first things you notice when you push your limits is the rush of dopamine. This is the brain’s way of rewarding you for taking on a challenge. It feels good, and it keeps you moving forward. But there is more to it than just feeling good. When you set a big goal and work towards it, you move much faster towards your long-term dreams. If you do not have a clear goal yet, now is the perfect time to create one. It can be anything—a dream you have always wanted to achieve, something that keeps you awake at night, or even a wild idea that seems out of reach.

Once you have your goal, the next step is to take action every day. This could mean doing research, reaching out to people who have already done what you want to do, or learning from companies and organizations in your field. Sometimes, it is about saving money for your project, even if it is just a small amount each day. For example, putting aside 50 or 100 euros daily, if possible, can make a big difference over time, especially for big dreams like going to space.

If your goal feels too big to handle alone, you can ask for help. Crowdfunding is a great way to bring people together who believe in your project. You might find a group of supporters who want to help you, whether it is for a cause, to honor someone, or to make a difference in the world. You can also ask your family and friends to contribute, maybe by creating a group fund and saving together over one or two years. Every little bit counts when you are working towards something that matters to you.

The most important thing is to keep moving forward, step by step. The journey is just as important as the destination. Each action you take brings you closer to your goal and helps you grow as a person.

## Key Takeaways
- Pushing your limits gives you energy and motivation.
- Set a clear, meaningful goal—even if it seems impossible.
- Take daily actions, no matter how small.
- Ask for help and build a support network.
- The journey shapes you as much as the result.

## Reflection
- What is one goal that scares you, but excites you at the same time?
- Who could you ask for support or advice on your journey?
- What small step can you take today to get closer to your dream?
