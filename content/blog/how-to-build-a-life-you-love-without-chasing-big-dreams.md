Have you ever thought about what it really means to build a life you love? It’s not just about chasing big dreams or ticking off goals. Sometimes, the first step is as simple as living in a place that truly suits you. The environment you choose can change everything.

You don’t need a fortune to move somewhere that makes you happy. Maybe you can relocate to another city or even another country. For example, many places in Asia offer a great quality of life at a much lower cost than you might expect. Personally, I live in Australia. It’s not the cheapest place—about as expensive as France—but I love it here. The vibe is fantastic, and the country is just amazing. But there are so many other options. New Zealand is a bit more affordable and just as wonderful. Costa Rica is another great choice. There are plenty of countries that are both beautiful and welcoming.

Of course, every place has its own quirks. Some are safer than others. Australia, for instance, is extremely safe. Sometimes, you even forget to be careful. But if you go back to Europe, you quickly remember not to leave your phone on the table when you step away. It’s just about staying aware and not leaving your valuables out in the open.

The real point is, you can live anywhere that feels right for you. Even if it’s just for a few weeks, like when I stayed in Dorothée for three weeks and absolutely loved it. The moment you find yourself in a place that lifts you up, everything else starts to fall into place. You’ll enjoy what you do more, you’ll feel more inspired, and you’ll do everything better. You’ll even help others more, just by being in the right environment.

This is why I believe so strongly in the power of your surroundings. You don’t need a fancy house or a big apartment. Even in a small 20-square-meter flat in the suburbs, you can create an environment that supports you. It’s all about making your space work for you, no matter where you are.

> *The environment you choose is the foundation for everything you build in your life.*
>
> — Pierre

When you take care of your environment, you’ll notice a huge difference in your well-being. You’ll feel better, happier, and you’ll get more done. It’s not magic—it’s just the quiet power of being in the right place.

## Key Takeaways
- *Your environment shapes your happiness and productivity.*
- *You don’t need a lot of money to live somewhere that inspires you.*
- *Even a small space can become your sanctuary if you make it your own.*
- *Being in the right place helps you do more and feel better.*

## Reflection
- *Are you living in a place that truly supports you?*
- *What small changes can you make to your environment today?*
- *How would your life change if you chose a place that lifts you up?*
