Growing in life starts with a clear vision. It’s about knowing what you want to achieve and daring to imagine a bigger, better version of your days. When you wake up in the morning, ask yourself: What am I going to do today? What will I accomplish? How can I change my routine, even just a little, to move closer to the life I dream of?

Most of us don’t feel great in our own skin. We don’t like our lives much, but we rarely ask ourselves the right questions. We don’t even stop to wonder: What would my ideal day look like? How could I change just one small thing today? You don’t need to change everything at once. Start with one thing, today. Then another tomorrow. Maybe take a moment every Sunday to look back at your week. What worked? What would you like to add or remove from your days? This simple habit of reflection can change everything.

Right now, I’m sitting in a cozy café in Seoul, a city I love for its warmth and the kindness of its people. Being here reminds me how important it is to ask yourself what really matters. Maybe you want to travel the world, live in Korea, learn a new language, become a pro at surfing or basketball, or see an NBA game in the US. What steps can you take right now? Maybe it’s as simple as saving a bit of money each week.

Too often, we make excuses. These excuses might make us feel better for a moment, but they keep us from acting. We stay stuck. If you want a different life, you can’t keep doing the same things. You have to act. Excuses are just ways to justify staying where you are. You might tell yourself, “I can’t do it because of this or that,” but if you keep thinking like that, you’ll never get what you want.

There’s never a perfect time to start. The best time is now. There’s a Chinese proverb that says the best time to plant a tree was twenty years ago. The second-best time is today. If you plant that tree now, in twenty years it will be strong and beautiful. It’s the same for your life. The best moment to grow is today, not tomorrow.

I believe in you. Go for it. Take action. Grow. We grow together.

> *The best time to plant a tree was twenty years ago. The second-best time is now.*
> — Chinese Proverb

## Key Takeaways
- *Ask yourself what your ideal day looks like.*
- *Change one small thing today, not everything at once.*
- *Reflect weekly on what’s working and what isn’t.*
- *Stop making excuses. The best time to act is now.*

## Action Steps
- *Write down one thing you want to change this week.*
- *Set aside time each Sunday to review your progress.*
- *Challenge your excuses and take one step forward today.*

## Reflection
- *What’s one dream you’ve been putting off?*
- *What’s stopping you from starting today?*
- *How would your life look if you made just one small change right now?*
