Being passionate about what you do—loving and enjoying every little thing you create—starts with simply doing something you like. That’s the heart of it. A passion project, or a passion you turn into a job, doesn’t have to begin with something huge. It can start as a tiny, almost invisible spark—something you do each day that brings you a bit of joy.

Maybe you have a regular job, a desk, a freelance mission that doesn’t really excite you. But alongside that, you nurture a micro-passion. Every day, you look for ways to grow it, even if it’s just a little. Sometimes, we get stuck with our heads down, focused only on the next task. But what you really need is the big picture—the architect’s view. Ask yourself each day: how can I improve this? What can I do to make this passion a bigger part of my life?

Little by little, this micro-passion can become something more important. It doesn’t mean it will take up more of your time, but you’ll do it better and more efficiently. That’s what makes all the difference for your future. In reality, you’re already investing in yourself.

Even if you only spend two hours a week on this passion, it can eventually become your full-time job. But when that happens, it won’t feel like a job—it will be your passion-job. That’s the real goal: to make your work something you truly enjoy.

At the end of the day, the purpose of life is to be happy. It really is that simple.

*TL;DR / Key Takeaways:*
- Start small: even a tiny passion can grow into something bigger.
- Keep the big picture in mind—don’t get lost in daily routines.
- Invest in yourself by making time for what you love, even if it’s just a little.
- Over time, your passion can become your main job, and it won’t feel like work.
- The real goal is happiness—don’t lose sight of that.

*Actions List:*
- Identify one small thing you love doing—do it every day.
- Each day, ask yourself how you can make it a bigger part of your life.
- Don’t wait for the “perfect moment”—start now, even if it’s just for a few minutes.

*Ask Yourself:*
- What’s one micro-passion you could start today?
- How can you make it grow, step by step?
- Are you living for your happiness, or just for your job?
